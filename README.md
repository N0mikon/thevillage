# The Village - Peasant Management Game

A village-themed incremental/idle game where you manage a growing settlement of peasants.

## Features

- **Resource Management**: Gather and manage Food, Wood, Stone, Knowledge, and Gold
- **Peasant Population**: Recruit and manage your peasant workforce
- **Building System**: Construct buildings to automate resource production
- **Job Assignment**: Assign peasants to different jobs for specialized production
- **Upgrades**: Research improvements to boost efficiency
- **Village Exploration**: Explore the surrounding area to find resources
- **Save/Load System**: Automatic saving with manual export/import options

## How to Play

1. **Start by gathering resources** - Click the "Gather" buttons to manually collect Food, Wood, and Stone
2. **Recruit peasants** - Use the "Recruit" button to grow your population
3. **Build structures** - Construct Houses for more peasant capacity, Farms for food production, etc.
4. **Assign jobs** - Give your peasants specific roles like Farmer, Lumberjack, or Miner
5. **Research upgrades** - Spend Knowledge to unlock improvements
6. **Explore** - Click on the village grid to explore new areas

## Game Systems

### Resources
- **Food**: Needed to feed peasants and assign Farmers
- **Wood**: Used for construction and Lumberjack jobs
- **Stone**: Required for buildings and Miner jobs
- **Knowledge**: Gained from Scholars, used for upgrades
- **Gold**: Earned by Merchants, used for advanced upgrades

### Buildings
- **House**: Increases peasant capacity (+2 per house)
- **Farm**: Automatically produces food
- **LumberMill**: Automatically produces wood
- **Quarry**: Automatically produces stone
- **Library**: Automatically produces knowledge
- **Market**: Automatically produces gold
- **Storage**: Increases resource storage capacity

### Jobs
- **Farmer**: Gathers food (+2/sec per farmer)
- **Lumberjack**: Chops wood (+2/sec per lumberjack)
- **Miner**: Mines stone (+2/sec per miner)
- **Scholar**: Gains knowledge (+2/sec per scholar)
- **Merchant**: Earns gold (+2/sec per merchant)
